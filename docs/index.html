My Creative Process

All my projects follow this method

2

Brainstorming Concept

After understanding the requirements, you have to land the client problems into a wireframe. It is necesary to ensure that what we are doing, is what the client needs

3

Software Development

Then you divide the project into sprints. In each sprint is important show the product to the client for receiving his feedback

4

Satisfaction

If we followed the last steps. We will have a happy client.
